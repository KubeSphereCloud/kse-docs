---
title: "查看 Supervisor 日志"
weight: 1
---

. 逐个登录集群节点。

. 执行如下命令，查看完整的 Supervisor 日志文件，检查有无反复重启的服务。
+
[,shell]
----
vi /var/log/supervisor/supervisord.log
----
+
若观察如下特特征的日志，即相关服务反复重启 3 次，需联系 NeonSAN 产研团队。
+
[,shell]
----
INFO exited: neoncenter (terminated by SIGINT/SIGTERM/SIGKILL; not expected) 
INFO exited: neoncenter (exit status <exit_status_code>; not expected) 
INFO exited: neonstore (terminated by SIGSEGV (core dumped); not expected)
----
