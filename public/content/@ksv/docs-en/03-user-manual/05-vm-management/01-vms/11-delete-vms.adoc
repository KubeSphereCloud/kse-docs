== Delete a VMlink:[id=a0a1804577a647268a59c7197c64ddb2]

include::../../../../_custom-en/vms/vm-view-steps.adoc[]

. On the **VMs** page, click the name of a VM to view its details.
. On the left side of the details page, click image:/images/ksv/en/icons/more.svg[operation-menu,13] and select **Delete** from the drop-down list.
. In the dialog box that appears, click **OK**.
+
--
include::../../../../../_ks_components/admonitions/warning.adoc[]

The VM cannot be restored after it is deleted. Exercise caution when you perform this operation.
