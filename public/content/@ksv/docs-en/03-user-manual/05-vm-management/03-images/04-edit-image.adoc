== Procedurelink:[id=46e258fa429c441d9e9072bc00011ad1]

include::../../../../_custom-en/vms/vm-image-steps.adoc[]

. On the **System Images**, **Private Images**, or **Shared Images** tab, view the images of a specific type. 
. On the **Images** page, click the name of the image that you want to view. The details page of the image appears.
. In the upper-left corner of the image details page, click **Edit**.
