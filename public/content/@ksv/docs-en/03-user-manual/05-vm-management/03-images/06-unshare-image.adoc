== Procedurelink:[id=632c68385cfd48ab93094a2a89e04ce8]

include::../../../../_custom-en/vms/vm-image-steps.adoc[]

. On the **Shared Images** tab, click image:/images/ksv/en/icons/more.svg[operation-menu,13] next to the image that you want to unshare, and select **Unshare** from the drop-down list.
. In the dialog box that appears, click **OK**.  After you stop sharing the image, you cannot replicate the image to other projects. This operation does not affect the images that have been replicated.
