== Procedurelink:[id=959950457d8e4a1f96a72b300299cdcb]

include::../../../_custom-en/disks/disk-steps.adoc[]

. On the **Disks** page, click the name of the desired disk. The details page of the disk appears.
. On the **Snapshots** tab, click **Create** in the upper-right corner.
. In the dialog box that appears, set **Name** and **Description** for the snapshot.
+

. Click **OK**.  The snapshot appears in the snapshot list after it is created.
