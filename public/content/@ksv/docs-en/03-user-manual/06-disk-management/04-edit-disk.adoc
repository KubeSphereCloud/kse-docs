== Procedurelink:[id=caa64fd9cf634f8b973523c418f104b5]

include::../../../_custom-en/disks/disk-steps.adoc[]

. Find the disk that you want to edit, click image:/images/ksv/en/icons/more.svg[operation-menu,13] on the right side, and then select **Edit** from the drop-down list.
. In the dialog box that appears, modify **Name** and **Description** of the disk, and click **OK**.
