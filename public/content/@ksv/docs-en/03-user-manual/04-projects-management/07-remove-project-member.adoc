== Procedurelink:[id=3fc2f8d948694fa6ba1e982a2f9ac2a0]

include::../../../_custom-en/projects/project-manage-steps.adoc[]

. On the **Projects** page, click the name of the project that you want to manage. The details page of the project appears.
. On the right side of the project details page, click the **Members** tab.
. Find the member that you want to remove, click image:/images/ksv/en/icons/more.svg[operation-menu,13] on the right side, and then select **Remove** from the drop-down list.
. In the dialog box that appears, click **OK**.
