This section introduces how to delete a license.

[.admon.warning,cols="a"]
|===
|Warning

|After the license is deleted, you will lose the authorization for the extension. Please proceed with caution.

|===

== Steps

include::../../../../_custom-en/platformManagement/platformManagement-oper-logIn.adoc[]

. Click image:/images/ks-qkcp/zh/icons/cogwheel.svg[cogwheel,18,18] in the upper-right corner of the page to enter the **Platform Settings** page.

+

include::../../../../../_ks_components-en/oper-navigate.adoc[]

. Click the **Licenses Management** tab to view the added license information.

. Click image:/images/ks-qkcp/zh/icons/trash-light.svg[trash-light,18,18] on the right side of the license you want to delete.

. In the **Delete License** dialog box, enter the name of the extension, and then click **OK**.
