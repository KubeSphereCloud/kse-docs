This section explains how to view and manage licenses for KubeSphere Enterprise and extensions.

KubeSphere Enterprise License is used to activate the KubeSphere Enterprise platform. To purchase or renew the license, please contact the KubeSphere Enterprise support team.

Extension License is used to activate certain extensions that require a purchase. Here, only the authorization information of extensions are displayed.
For more information about extension licenses, please visit the extension details page in the Extensions Center and contact the extension providers for support.
