== Steps

include::../../../_custom-en/platformManagement/platformManagement-oper-logIn.adoc[]

. Click image:/images/ks-qkcp/zh/icons/cogwheel.svg[cogwheel,18,18] in the top right corner of the {ks_product-en} web console to access the **Platform Settings** page.

+

include::../../../../_ks_components-en/oper-navigate.adoc[]
+
--
The **Platform Information** page displays the platform's access address, title, description, logo, browser icon, and the background image on the login page.
--

. Click **Customize Platform Information** in the top-right corner of the page.

. In the **Customize Platform Information** dialog box, set the platform's information as desired, and then click **OK**.
+
--
[%header,cols="1a,4a"]
|===
|Parameter |Description

|Platform Title
|The name of the platform. The platform title can contain any characters and can be up to 63 characters long.

|Platform Description
|A description of the platform. The description can contain any characters and can be up to 256 characters long.

|Platform Logo
|The logo displayed at the top of the web console interface. The logo can be in SVG, PNG, or JPG format and should not exceed 2 MB. Recommended resolution is 200 x 40 pixels.

|Browser Favicon
|The icon displayed on the browser tab. The icon can be in ICO, SVG, PNG, or JPG format and should not exceed 2 MB. Recommended resolution is 40 x 40 pixels with a 1:1 aspect ratio.

|Login Page Background
|The background image for the platform's login page. The image can be in SVG, PNG, or JPG format and should not exceed 2 MB. Recommended resolution is 1920 x 1080 pixels.
|===
